This is the Lechon Manok recipe!

What is Lechon Manok and where does it originate from?

Lechon manok is a Filipino spit-roasted chicken dish made with chicken marinated in a mixture of garlic, bay leaf, onion, black pepper, soy sauce, and patis.

The marinade may also be sweetened with muscovado or brown sugar. It is stuffed with tanglad and roasted over charcoal.

So what are the ingredients?

  • 1 Whole chicken
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
    • Onion
  • Butter
  • Bay leaves(optional)
  • Spices and Seasonings!

Step by Step process in making our Lechon Manok

  1. Slice the lemon, garlic, onion, and stuff it inside the chicken! (this creates beautiful flavor and juices inside the chicken.)
  2. Gently lift the chicken skin (do not rip the skin off) and put butter inside it.
  3. And lastly we are going to grill it for about 30 minutes on medium heat

Then voila! our Lechon Manok is complete!!
