This is the Adobo Recipe!

What is adobo?

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish and cooking process in Philippine cuisine that involves meat, seafood, or vegetables marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns, which is browned in oil, and simmered in the marinade.

This Chicken adobo recipe has tender, juicy chicken cooked in a rich, flavorful peppery sauce that's simple to make and delicious served over rice.

Ingredients we will be using.

  • 1 lbs pork or chicken meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Here are the steps to making this amazing dish!

  1. Slice the onion and garlic! It depends on you if you want minced, big slices or not!
  2. Saute the onion and garlic with butter or oil.
  3. Add the chicken or pork meat.
  4. Let it cook for a few minutes until it slightly turns brown.
  5. Then you can add the soy sauce and vinegar.
  6. You can also add bay leaves if you want!
